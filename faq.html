Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Recommended Age?

While nothing in our rooms are considered inappropriate, they are specifically designed for older teens and adults. Generally they are for high school and up. Youth 14 and under must be accompanied by at least one (1) paying and participating adult. Children 12 and under will only be allowed in private room.

Is It Considered Scary?

Our rooms were not created with the purpose of being scary, though they are fun and exhilarating. A great experience for adrenaline junkies!

How Long Does the Experience Last?

Arriving on time, plan for your escaping experience to take at least 60 minutes or less (depending on if you escape!)

Can I Take Photos and Videos Inside the Rooms?

Cell phones are not allowed in our rooms at any time. Photography and video recordings are also prohibited. Ask a staff member if you would like to take a photo during your experience, they will be happy to help!

Can We Bring Outside Food and Beverages in With Us?

We do not allow and food or beverages in our rooms at any time. Although we provide bottles of water for our guests, you may not take them in the rooms with you.

Are You Handicap Accessable?

Yup! We are handicap accessible. We have a wheelchair ramp for both entrances to the building. Please contact us if you have any other questions about handicap accessibility.

Will We Be Recorded in the Rooms?

Our cameras are purely to ensure your safety during your experience, and so our employees can keep track of how you’re doing in case you need a hint. The cameras provide a live video feed, but they do not save a recording of you.

Can I Book a Room as Private?

Yes, you can reserve a room as private. Otherwise, we cannot guarantee who will be in the room with you. It’s a great experience to meet new people!

Is There Anywhere You Won't Take Your Mobile Trailers?

There aren’t too many paces we won't take our Mobile trailers. Apartment buildings, parks, and public locations (without the owner’s consent) are the only exceptions. Find out more about where we’ll go and booking Mobile rooms on our Mobile page!
