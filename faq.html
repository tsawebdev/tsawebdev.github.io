Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended age?

While nothing in our rooms are considered inappropriate, they are specifically designed for older teens and adults. Generally they are for high school and up. Youth 18 and under must be accompanied by at least one (1) paying a participating adult. Children 12 and under will only be allowed in private rooms.


Is it scary?

Our rooms are not designed to be scary, though they are fun and exhilarating. A great experience for adrenaline junkies!


How long does it take?

Arriving on time, plan for your experience to take at least 60 minutes or less (depending on if you escape!)


Can I take photos and videos inside the rooms?

Cell phones are not allowed in our rooms at any time. Photography and video recording are not permitted at any time. Ask a staff member if you would like to take a photo during your experience, they will be happy to help!


Can I bring outside food and beverages in with me?

We do not allow and food or beverages in our rooms at any time.


Are you handicap accessible?

Yup! We are handicap accessible. We have a wheelchair ramp for both entrances to the building. Please contact us if you have any other questions about handicap accessibility.


Will I be recorded in the rooms?

Our cameras are purely to ensure your safety during your experience, and so our employees can keep track of how you’re doing in case you need a hint. The cameras provide a live video feed, but they do not save a recording of you.


Can I book a room as private?

Yes, you can reserve a room as private. Otherwise, we cannot guarantee who will be in the room with you. It’s a great experience to meet new people!


Is there anywhere you won't go?

Other than the obvious, There’s not too many paces we won't go. Apartment buildings and parks (without owner’s consent) are the only two places. On The Run Escape Rooms are great for birthday parties, office parties, team building exercises, or just a fun day!
