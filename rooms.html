OUR ROOMS

Below you will find our three, fun-filled experiences we offer here at On The Run Escape Rooms located at our HQ. The rooms we bring to you - yes, you read that right, we’ll bring the fun to you! - are listed under the Mobile page.

Epidemic

Your first day as an epidemiologist is nothing short of insane - a deadly virus has broken out and it's up to you and up to 11 others to save the world! Build the cure and distribute it to the rest of the word before time runs out - or perish trying!

6-12 people

Not recomended for groups of less than 6!

Hacker

The world power grid is under attack! In about 60 minutes, the whole world could lose power, in turn, destroying businesses and crashing the economy! Use your smarts to out-hack the hacker and save the world power grid!

4-6 people

Murder Mystery

There’s a serial killer loose in your neighborhood! You set up traps to protect yourself in your home, but instead trap yourself inside with him! Quickly, you must escape your own home or face the serial killer head on.

5-10 People

Pricing

Prices of these rooms will vary with the amount of people that will participate. For all rooms:

  • Parties with 6-10 people

    $24.99

  • Parties with 10-12 people

    $29.99

  • Parties with 12-25 people

    $39.99


If you are having an office party and you have 12-25 people participating we will provide a discount of $5.00.

For our mobile rooms, We also charge for miles driven to cover the cost of gas. Expect to play around $0.50 a mile, so keep this into consideration when planning a party.

Office party escape rooms are very fun and enjoyable for all employees in the office and, as stated earlier, are great team building exercises capable of strengthening your office as a whole. We always make sure our rooms are affordable for everybody to enjoy!
